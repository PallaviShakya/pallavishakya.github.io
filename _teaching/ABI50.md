I was a Teaching Assistant for Animal Biology Laboratory (ABI50A) in the Fall Quarter of 2023. This is an undergraduate course where students ask their own research questions on a model organism (Centrachid fishes) and conduct short population biology and field experiments to answer those questions. 

## Responsibilities
* Prepare laboratory materials and set up dissection and microscopy stations.
* Assist students in designing and conducting experiments.
* Help students with data analysis (using R and excel sheet) and interpretation. 
* Grade proposals, research reports and exams.
* Help instructor in sample collection and field trip.
